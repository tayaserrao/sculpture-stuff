The "Bocas del Dragon" is the name of the straits separating the Gulf of Paria from the Caribbean Sea. The Boca Grande or Grand Boca separates Chacachacare from the Paria Peninsula and Patos Island of Venezuela. The international border between Trinidad and Venezuela runs through this strait
BOCAS DEL DRAGON, North Coast, NW Trinidad 10°45'0"N, 61°46'0"W
Never dive in this area! Stay above the surface or try to find something to float! You can sometimes dive below the waves and avoid the bad currents but thats only if you are a really good swimmer. I did that once at Maracas and I ended up getting tumbled underwater so badly that I couldn't feel gravity anymore and I lost my alignment to the earth . When I found it again, swimming and finding the shore, I remember feeling like I had been hollowed out.
The water is great for most months of the year!
